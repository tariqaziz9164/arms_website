Government Compliance:

We take pride in our strict adherence to government regulations, holding essential certificates such as:

  • Form X (10): Authorizing the sale and purchase of Pistols and 12 Bore short guns and their ammunition
  • Form IX (12): Authorizing the sale and purchasing of NPB guns, breech loading guns, and ammunitions.
  • Form XIII (13): Repairing of all NPB pistols, 12 Bore, and Guns.
